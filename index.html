Convocation is a 2.5-day gathering of 150 people who are designing a college from first-principles.

An actual college — one with dorms, lecture halls, and labs.

College campuses are for sale all around the country, and we're buying one. We're building a higher education institution where the student experience is our only obsession.

Convocation is bringing together a group of people who are capable of rethinking what that student experience should be. Sessions will include live debates and discussions, interviews, talks, and interactive workshops.

The event will be conversation-focused: much of the attendee time will be dedicated to one-on-one and small-group conversations, and most sessions will be followed by office hours where attendees can engage more deeply with speakers.

Topics of focus will include business models for higher education, learning sciences, high-leverage specializations, core curriculum design, psychometrics, credentialing, game-based learning, human-computer interaction outcome-based assessments, peer-review, university governance, campus culture, student experience metrics, faculty evaluation, academic freedom, standard setting and benchmarking, mentorship, tutoring, publishing, and artificial intelligence.
