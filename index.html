Dear visitor,

Thank you for visiting our page.

The Assembly is a three-day gathering of 150 people who are designing a college from first-principles.

An actual college — one with dorms, lecture halls, and labs. College campuses are for sale all around the country.

And we don’t want to just preserve these spaces — we’re building a new institution of higher education where the student experience is our only obsession.

Sessions at The Assembly will include live debates and discussions, interviews, talks, and interactive workshops.

The event will be conversation-focused: much of the venue and attendee time will be dedicated to one-on-one and small-group conversations, and most sessions will be followed by office hours where attendees can engage more deeply with speakers.
