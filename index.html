Introduction to Convocation Labs

Dear visitor,

Research is science performed as art.

We believe the opportunity to learn this art isn't accessibel enough.

Convocation Labs is for 12 high school students to learn the craftsmanship necessary to work on hard problems in AI, specifically multi-agent planning & causal learning with interventions.

We want to help you develop a better research intuition so you can do more impactful work. Our intent is that by the end of this program, you will be a more elegant thinker and effective researcher. You will also have a paper ready for submission to conferences.

The program fee is a 2.5% FUTURE (“free university through upfront rights to equity"). The FUTURE means that Convocation is entitled to 2.5% of any company you become a founder/co-founder of within five years of Semester Zero.

The program fee is $5,000, we only work with 12 students at a time, and our 12-week schedule is as follows:

Two 2.5 hour sessions per week -- one whole-group discussion session on the art of research design & productivity, given by a rotating selection of instructors and one small-group technical session on your project, led by your group’s assigned mentor.

Mentors will be available on a text-basis and for 1:1 office hours. They can help with 1) literature review and ideation, 2) code implementation and model training, 3) drafting of your manuscript, 4) conference submission.
